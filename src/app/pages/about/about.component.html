About Us

Who we are

Established in 2002, we are a fully integrated plastic recycling company, being the FIRST company in the world to receive Ocean Bound Plastic OBP certification co-developed by NGO Zero Plastic Oceans and Control Union. We collect used ocean plastics and transform them into manufacturable materials that are just as good as new.

Our Clientele

More than 70% of our output has been exported to 35 countries worldwide. Our customer profiles are diverse; ranging from small and medium-sized startups to Fortune 500 companies.

Our Innovation

At HHI we are constantly improving ways to streamline our operations. As of now, we have 18 intellectual properties and 1 trademark filed, all of which we take great ownership in.

Our Certification

Our quality is recognised globally. Besides receiving ISO 9001, 14001 and 45001 certifications, we are GRS certified for its 100% recycled materials, and SMETA certified for social compliance.
OBP Certification Program is a model for companies to finance the collection of OBP, established by Zero Plastic Oceans (ZPO)
GRS is a global, voluntary, full product standard that sets requirements for third-party certification of recycled content, chain of custody, chemical restrictions, as well as social and environmental practices.
SEDEX/SMETA assesses the standard of the site based on its labour, health and safety, business and environmental ethics.
ISO 9001 is a global standard that specifies requirements for a quality management system (QMS)
ISO 14001 is a global standard that specifies requirements for an effective environmental management system (EMS)
ISO 45001 is a global standard for occupational health and safety, issued to protect employees and visitors from work-related accidents and diseases.

Why we do it

We believe that plastic is a resource, not a problem. We are looking for a better way of optimising plastic, that will prevent it from ending up in oceans and landfills.

Ocean plastic is overabundant

There is simply too much plastic being produced and too little of it being recycled. Although 320 million tonnes of global cumulative plastic is produced in 2015, over 91% of the plastics is not recycled.

Our marine life is in danger

Most of the plastic pollution in our oceans flows from the land, which are ingested by marine animals who mistake these fragments for food. This ultimately, puts our marine ecosystem and our health at risk.
13 billion dollars
of financial damage comes from the plastic pollution in the marine ecosystems. That is a hefty price to pay for our plastic consumption habit.

Our philosophy

We want to be a key player in being environmentally conscious, and work towards a cleaner future. The ABC pillars is our way of doing so.

Instead of having a linear process where products get manufactured and then disposed, we operate in the closed-loop process of reusing, recycling, and reprocessing; creating a circular economy.
Circular Economy
Following the cycle of the circular economy, plastic can be fully re-utlised for sustainable and long lasting outcomes.

Smart Plastics

Smart plastics is our customisable material made out of recycled plastics. Not only are they just as good, if not better than newly manufactured plastics, they are also designed to save cost, time, waste, and most importantly, our ocean.

We do not produce one-size-fits-all plastic. Our recycled plastics are fully customisable to fit all sorts of applications.
Faster, better, greener
We employ greener production processes in comparison to manufacturing using newly manufactured plastic. Since this is done through a single, streamlined supply chain, it saves production cost too.
Sustainable manufacturing
We are aware that consumers are now expecting a greater degree of social responsibility and transparency from businesses than ever before—which is why we want to help companies manufacture ethically. At HHI, we provide in-depth consultation and tailor the way we source our plastic materials based on the sustainability goals of our customers.
We offer smart plastic materials sourced from two types of recycled plastics.

Post-consumer recycled plastics

Plashaus is our trademarked smart plastic product, made out of generic recycled plastics collected from other sources before they end up in the landfill or the ocean.

Ocean-bound plastics

Plashaus Ocean is made out of sourced from plastic that are at high risk of entering the ocean if not collected in time, which includes shoreline, rivers, banks or landfill.
