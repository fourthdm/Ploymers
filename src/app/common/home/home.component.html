    About Us

    S.S.B. POLYMERS & S.S.B. ENTERPRISES is a privately owned dynamic company, founded in 2008 as an international reselling/trading company of industrial plastic raw materials. We have been performing in the plastic material industry since 2008 and we are mainly specialized in Nylon / ABS and Polypropylene raw material. Worldwide product sourcing and well established links with suppliers make EURSIACORP an excellent partner for plastic processors and converters. Very concerned by the future of our planet we also focus on regrinds and regran from post industrial and post consumer waste.




    Activities

    Manufacturing and Trading in Broad Range of Plastic Raw Material, Plastic Raw Material Reprocessing Services, Virgin Plastic Raw Material, Color Masterbatches, Colored Plastic Granules, Job Work of Reprocessing of Plastic, Master Batches Plastic, Plastic Raw, Materials, Polymer Granules, Polypropylene Granules, Pp Granules , Pvc Plastic Granules Recycled Granules, Reprocessed Plastic Granules, Reprocessors Of Plastic Raw Materials,Thermoplastic Granules, Thermoplastic Raw Materials


    Assurance

    Our world-class quality products that are manufactured by us. Our company is committed to manufacture Plastic Raw Materials.

    Our Products


    1. Nylon Granules :

    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Nylon Granules, Nylon 6 Unfilled Granules, Nylon 6 Glassfilled Granules, Nylon 66 Glassfilles Granules, Nylon 66 Granules, Nylon 66 Technyl Unfilled GlassFilled, Nylon 6 Virgin Reprocessed, Nylon Natural Black Unfilled GlassFilled and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    Nylon is the plastic material based on polyamide-6 that is a thermoplastic resin with excellent mechanical, thermal and electrical properties. Nylon is widely used in the manufacture of automobile and industrial parts.


    Range :

    • NYLON 6 UNFILLED
    • NYLON 6 GLASSFILLED
    • NYLON 66 UNFILLED
    • NYLON 66 GLASSFILLES







    2. Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) :


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene Granules, ABS Granules, Flame Retardant ABS Granules, ABS Natural Granules, ABS Extrusion Sheet Granules, ABS Virgin LG Bhansali Absolac Sabic Starex Chimei, ABS Reprocessed Black Dana Granules, ABS FR V0 V2 Black Natural and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    ABS is a highly-functional plastic known for its strong heat resistance and impact resistance. It is highly processable and can be manufactured in various colors. It is used in numerous areas including toys such as automobiles, home appliances, IT devices, etc.


    Range :

    • ABS BLACK
    • ABS NATURAL
    • ABS FLAME RETARDENT
    • ABS PLATING





    3. Polypropylene (PP)


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Polypropylene Granules, PP Granules, PPCP Black, Yellow, Blue,Green Granules, PP Talc Filled Granules, PP Glass Filled Granules, PPCP Reprocessed Black and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    Complete range of Homo, Random & Impact Polypropylene and Polypropylene Compounds for Flexible and Rigid Applications.


    Range :

    • PPCP BLACK
    • PPCP YELLOW, RED, BLUE, GREY, GREEN
    • PP BLACK
    • PP TALC FILLED
    • PP GLASS FILLED





    4. Polyoxymethylene (POM)


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Polyoxymethylene Granules, POM Plastic Granules and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    POM is a material of an excellent quality with features of excellent anti-friction/anti-wear, chemical-resistance, heat-resisting stability, precise dimensions and moulding abilities. It is mainly applied in gear or roller, and is used for various purposes over the fields of cars, office equipment and living materials.


    Range :

    • POM COPOLYMER
    • POM HOMOPOLYMER





    5. Polycarbonates (Pc)


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Polycarbonate Granules, PC Granules, Polycarbonate Natural Granules, Polycarbonate Black Granules and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    Polycarbonate, an amorphous polymer, offers a unique combination of toughness with exceptional impact strength. It also exhibits high optical clarity, excellent creep, and high temperature resistance.


    Range :

    • Polycarbonate Natural
    • Polycarbonate Black
    • PC / ABS





    6. Poly Methyl Methacrylate (PMMA)


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Polymethyl Methacrylate Granules, PMMA Plastic Granules, PMMA Granules, PMMA Natural Granules, PMMA Red Granules and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    Super transparent, amorphous PMMA offers high resistance to UV light and weathering, chemical and heat resistance, long service life, and unlimited colouring options for various applications.


    Range :

    • PMMA NATURAL
    • PMMA RED





    7. Poly Vinyl Chloride (PVC)


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Poly Vinyl Chloride Granules, PVC Granules, PVC Black Granules, Natural PVC Granules, PVC Colored Granules, PVC Transparent Granules and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    A durable and long-lasting material that can be used in a variety of applications, either rigid or flexible: building and construction, medical devices and food packaging.


    Range :

    • PVC BLACK
    • PVC NATURAL
    • PVC COLOUR
    • PVC TRANSPARENT COLOUR





    8. Polystyrene (PS)


    We are Manufacturer, Supplier, Exporter of Polystyrene Granules, PS Granules, HIPS Granules, GPPS Granules and our setup is situated in Pune, Maharashtra, India.

    A transparent polymer used in a variety of applications, such as food packaging and appliances. Polystyrene foams are used for thermal insulation and protective packaging.


    Range :

    • HIPS
    • GPPS
